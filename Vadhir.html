About

Vadhir Derbez, born on February 18, 1991, in Mexico City, is a talented Mexican actor and singer, best known as the son of renowned comedian Eugenio Derbez. He began his career as a child actor at age six, appearing in Televisa sketches and later gaining acclaim as the winner of Univision's debut season of Mira Quién Baila in 2010. He went on to star in series like Gossip Girl: Acapulco and La querida del Centauro, and transitioned to Hollywood with roles including the comedy How to Be a Latin Lover. Vadhir has also pursued music, releasing singles such as "Latin Lover."
